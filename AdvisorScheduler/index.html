Adding and Changing Advisor Scheduler Availability for Mac OS X and Windows
Important Note: The web application of the scheduler is for students. As an advisor, you will be doing all of your work in Microsoft Outlook.
Things may appear slightly differently between windows and mac, however, the content remains the same

Hovering over the blue highlighted text opens an image showing what to do

Initial Setup

1. Open Microsoft Outlook on your computer
a. It may appear on your dock, desktop, or applications menu
2. In the bottom left-hand corner of the window,
click on the "Calendar" button
3. Along the bar of icons at the top of the window,
select the option "Calendar Permissions"
4. In the window that popped up,
click "Add..."
5. In the search bar at the top,
type in "University College Advisors"
6. Select the top option and click "OK" at the bottom of the window
7.
Select "University College Advisors"
from the list of Calendars
a. In the "Read" section below,
select "Full Details"
and then click "OK" at the bottom of the window
