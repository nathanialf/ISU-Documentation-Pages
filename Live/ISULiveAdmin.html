Using the ISU Live Administration Tool

Hovering over the blue highlighted text opens an image showing what to do

Terminologies

ISU Live Player
: Any computer that is running Windows and ISU Live
ISU Live TV
: TV / Monitor connected to the ISU Live Player and displays the contents of ISU Live
ISU Live Admin Site
: The website with controlled access that allows departments with ISU Live Players to add and update ISU Live contents
ISU Live Administrator
: Department personnel with an ISU Live admin account to update the departmental contents in ISU Live. Please contact MDRPS for an ISU Live Administrator account

Getting Signed In

1. Go to:
discovery.indstate.edu/~isulive/admin
a. URL will change before full release. When that happens, it will be updated here.
2. Sign in with you University Username (formerly known as Portal ID) and password and select the department you belong to.
3. If you are setting up an ISU Live Player for the first time, you will want to go through all the sections and make sure everything is working as you want it to.

Layouts

1. On the sidebar,
click "Layout"
2.
Select the layout
option you want
3.
Press "Set Layout"
4. On the right side of the layout you decided, under "Contents",
click "Set Contents"
5. Choose whatever you want to go
in the labelled positions
and click the "Set Department Contents" button

Resolution

1. On the sidebar,
click "Resolution"
2.
Select the resolution of the television that this will be displayed on
a. If you do not know what the resolution of the television, contact someone who may know or that can figure it out for you.
3.
click "Set Resolution"

Contents

NOTE: Any text you manually enter as content supports HTML formatting. It is recommended that you do not attempt to style it if you do not know how to use HTML.
1. On the sidebar,
Click "News Text"
or
Click "RSS Feed"
or any other options in those places
2. To add news, in the top right corner,
click "Add News" (or "Add RSS Link", etc.)
a. Fill in the information to whatever it is that you want to be displayed
3. To edit news, along the right side of the news item,
click the pencil icon
4. To remove news, along the right side of the news item,
click the trash can icon
5. This information will take at least an hour to update itself on the televisions that are currently on

Preview the displays

1. On the sidebar,
click "Admin View"
